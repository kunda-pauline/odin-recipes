Topped Ice Cream Dessert

Description

This ice cream cake is the perfect frozen dessert with fudge, caramel and salty pretzels

INGREDIENTS

  • CRUST
  • ICE CREAM
  • FUDGE LAYER
  • TOPPING

STEPS

  1. Combine all crust ingredients in bowl
  2. Spread ice cream over crust. Cover
  3. Meanwhile, combine all fudge layer ingredients
  4. Pour cooled fudge layer mixture over frozen ice cream
